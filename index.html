Paws And Whiskers Shelter

An initiative to help the voiceless.

About Us

According to the reports there are about 30 million stray dogs in India without food,shelter and veterinary care. While some just abandon their pets.

So PAWS helps in fighting these problems and help these stray animals by running a regular drive,feeding these stray dogs and helping them find someone who can foster them.

What we do for them

  • FOOD DRIVE:

    We collect food from the people willing to donate food. Then a team of 4-5 members find the stray dogs and provide them with the same.

  • MEDICATION:

    Several stray dogs and cats are hit/injured and left unattended and so we try our best to find them and the medical team gives the aid accordingly. Other than that the team also provides the stray animals with anti-rabies injection.

  • ADOPTION:

    Many people are not able to take care of their pets and eventually end up abandoning them. We have animal shelter and promote adoption of dogs/cats.You can contact us via email if you have or anyone around you wants to surrender or adopt dogs or cats.
